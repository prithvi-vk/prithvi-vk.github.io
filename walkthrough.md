These are real spaces, captured as **Gaussian splats** — millions of tiny coloured blobs that blur into something photoreal from every angle. **Drag** to look around, **WASD** (or the on-screen controls) to move. They're other people's captures, embedded the way [SuperSplat](https://github.com/playcanvas/supersplat) intends — full credit to the creators linked under each.

_New here? Read the [post on how this works and how to make your own →](/blog/walk-through-a-room/)._
